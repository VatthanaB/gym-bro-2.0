  - Example mix: 600g chicken + 400g fish + 400g beef

**Additional proteins**:

- 28-35 eggs (4-5 per day for breakfast + snacks)

- 210g protein powder (optional, for shakes)

**Carbs**:

- 10 slices bread OR 350g oats (or mix)

**Veg**:

**Oils & extras**:

### **Sunday Mega Prep (3 hours)**

1. **Boil 14 eggs** → breakfast/snacks for week
2. **Cook lunch proteins**:
   - 1.2kg chicken (for 3 lunches)
   - 800g beef (for 2 lunches)
   - Keep 800g raw fish/prawns in fridge (cook fresh or Wed prep)
3. **Cook dinner proteins**:
   - 600g chicken (for 3 dinners)
   - 400g beef (for 2 dinners)
   - Keep 400g fish raw (cook fresh or Wed prep)
4. **Cook 420g dry carbs** (6 meals worth)
   - Portion into 6 containers (70-80g cooked each)
5. **Prep all vegetables**:
   - Wash, chop, portion into daily containers
6. **Portion dairy**:
   - 200g containers of cottage cheese/yogurt
   - Label for specific days

---

### **Wednesday Mid-Week Prep (45 min)**

1. Cook remaining fish/prawns for lunches (if not doing fresh)
2. Cook remaining proteins for dinners
3. Boil 7 more eggs if running low
4. Cook remaining 140g dry carbs
5. Check veg inventory, prep more if needed

## **TRACKING TIPS FOR 600g MEAT PLAN**

1. **Weigh ALL proteins raw before cooking**:

   - Lunch: 400g raw (→ becomes ~295g cooked)
   - Dinner: 200g raw (→ becomes ~145g cooked)

2. **Log in Yazio as RAW weights**:

   - "Chicken breast, raw: 400g" (lunch)
   - "Chicken breast, raw: 200g" (dinner)

3. **Carbs: Always log DRY weight**:

   - "White rice, dry: 80g" (not cooked weight)

4. **If meal prepping cooked chicken**:

   - Option A: Weigh 400g raw, cook it, store it, log as 400g raw ✅
   - Option B: Know that ~295g cooked ≈ 400g raw, log as 400g raw ✅

5. **Weekly check**:
   - 7 days × 600g = 4.2kg meat per week
   - Count your packages to verify
