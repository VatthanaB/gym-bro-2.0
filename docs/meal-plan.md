# SIMPLE SWAP MEAL PLAN

**Target**: 1,900 cal | 170g protein | Same structure daily

---

## **BREAKFAST** (450 cal, 35g protein) - ULTRA SIMPLE

**Pick ONE combo**:

**Option A**: 3 eggs + 1 toast
**Option B**: 3 eggs + 40g oats
**Option C**: 2 eggs + 1 toast + 100g Greek yogurt
**Option D**: Omelette (3 eggs + veg) + ½ toast

**Add if hungry**: Black coffee or green tea (0 cal)

_Same every day if you want._

---

## **SNACK 1** (200 cal, 25g protein) - 10-11am

**Pick ONE**:

- **A**: 150g cottage cheese
- **B**: 150g Greek yogurt (0% fat)
- **C**: 100g cottage cheese + 50g Greek yogurt
- **D**: Protein shake (25g powder + water)

**Optional add**: 50g berries or cucumber

---

## **LUNCH** (650 cal, 60g protein) - 12-2pm

**Formula**: PROTEIN + CARB + VEG + OIL

**PROTEIN** - Pick ONE (300g raw):

- Chicken breast

- Tuna steak
- Lean beef
- Turkey breast
- White fish
- Prawns

**CARB** - Pick ONE:

- 70g dry rice (white or brown)
- 70g dry pasta

- 70g dry couscous

**VEG** - Pick ONE or mix (200g raw):

- Broccoli
- Mixed salad
- Peppers + onions
- Zucchini
- Tomatoes + cucumber
- Green beans
- Spinach

**OIL**: 8ml (½ tbsp) for cooking/dressing

---

## **SNACK 2** (200 cal, 25g protein) - 4-5pm

**PROTEIN** - Pick ONE (150g raw):

- Chicken breast

- Lean beef/steak

**VEG** - Pick ONE or mix (300g raw):

- Broccoli

- Asparagus
- Zucchini
- Spinach

**OIL**: 5ml (1 tsp) for cooking

---

## SWAP RULES

✅ **Within each category, swap freely**:

- Any protein for any protein (same weight)
- Any carb for any carb (same weight)
- Any veg for any veg
- Cottage cheese ↔ Greek yogurt ↔ Protein shake

✅ **Keep the structure**:

- Breakfast: Always eggs + smaller carb
- Lunch: Always big protein + carb + veg
- Dinner: Always medium protein + veg (NO CARBS)

✅ **Don't swap between meals**:

- Don't move lunch carbs to dinner
- Don't skip protein at any meal
- Keep portions as listed

---

## WEEKLY PREP STRATEGY

**Sunday prep** (2 hours):

1. Boil 10 eggs → breakfast/snacks sorted
2. Cook 1kg protein for lunches (4-5 days worth)
3. Cook 900g protein for dinners (6 days worth)
4. Cook 490g dry rice/pasta (portion into 7 containers)
5. Chop veg, store in containers

**Wednesday mini-prep** (30 min):

- Cook remaining proteins
- Prep remaining veg

**Grab and go**:

- Pre-portioned cottage cheese/yogurt in small containers
- Hard boiled eggs ready
- Protein shakes (just add water)
