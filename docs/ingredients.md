## NOTES ON MEASUREMENT

### Cooking Weight Changes:

- **Proteins**: Typically lose 25-30% weight when cooked (mostly water)
- **Rice**: Gains 200-300% weight (absorbs water)
- **Pasta**: Gains 140-150% weight (absorbs water)
- **Vegetables**: Minimal change, slight weight loss

### Portion Tips:

- **300g raw chicken** → ~220g cooked (still log as 300g raw)
- **70g dry rice** → ~210g cooked (log as 70g dry)
- **200g raw vegetables** → ~180-190g cooked

### Tracking Best Practices:

1. **Weigh ingredients RAW for proteins** (before cooking)
2. **Weigh carbs DRY** (rice, pasta, oats before cooking)
3. **Vegetables can be weighed raw or cooked** (minimal difference)
4. **Track oils/fats by volume** (ml) or weight (g)
